About Me

I am Tanmayee, a computer science and engineering undergraduate from BVRIT HYDERABAD College of Engineering for Women. I am a passionate, outgoing person with many dreams and goals. I am currently self-learning about user experience and user interaction design.

View my resume here.




As a part of the Women Engineers program (run by Talentsprint and sponsored by Google), I had the opportunity to attend a design thinking webinar, which introduced the concept of UX design and designing for inclusivity for me. I was enthralled by the idea of being able to come up with experiences that leave lasting impacts on people, or make their daily lives easier. I started my learning process in the end of 2020, whilst handling my academics and extracurricular activities alongside.

I am an avid reader, and have my own library of around two hundred books. I love reading books of the fantasy genre, especially if the book toes the line between fantasy and reality with grace. I am also a poet, and a couple of poems have been published in school and college blogs and newspapers. In my free time, I love to kick back and watch historical documentaries.
A fun fact about me is that I am teaching myself Spanish!
