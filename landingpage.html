Landing Page

I believe that creating a first impression that clings to someone for a long time is extremely important. When this first impression is made through a screen, with written words to stand for who and what you are, the stakes get higher. As someone who has just started learning about user experience and interaction design, I wanted to try my hand at making an impactful and memorable first impression for someone who we often see on the internet; a blogger. I decided to design a landing page for a blogger.

Solo design

These days, blogs are found in abundance, and often operate on a common theme. I had some difficulties trying to make the landing page as unique as possible while staying true to a tried and tested layout. I played with color palettes, themes, and layouts; some seemed entirely too subtle, while others were the complete opposite.My idea was to make the landing page the protagonist of the movie; having some traits that any other protagonist would have, but at the same time retaining its uniqueness.
